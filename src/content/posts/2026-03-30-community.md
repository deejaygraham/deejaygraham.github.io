I often think one of the great weaknesses of our era is that we get
lone superhero movies over and over that suggest that our big problems 
are solved by muscly guys in spandex whose superpower is the ability 
to inflict and endure extraordinary violence. When actually the world 
mostly gets changed through collective effort. It brings up something 
really beautiful that Thich Nhat Hanh said: 'the next Buddha will be 
the Sangha.' The Sangha in Buddhist terminology is the community of 
practitioners.
